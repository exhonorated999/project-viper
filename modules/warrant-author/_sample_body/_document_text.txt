IDENTIFICATION AND EXPERTISE OF AFFIANT:
Your affiant, Bradley Guith, has been a sworn police officer since May, 1996 with the Los Angeles Police Department.  Your affiant changed employment to the Fontana Police Department in 2001.  Your affiant is currently assigned to the Fontana Police Department Investigations Unit as a Crimes Against Property Detective.  Your affiant has participated in over 1000 various investigations and arrests including, but not limited to:  forgery, fraud, grand and petty theft, burglary, receiving and possession of stolen property, illegal narcotics offenses (sales, transportation, and possession), criminal street gang enforcement, weapons and firearms offenses, assault, aggravated assault, domestic violence, sexual exploitation of minors, child abuse, rape, robbery, and homicide.

Your affiant has attended a basic P.O.S.T. academy at the Los Angeles Police Academy, where your affiant received approximately 960 hours of instruction.  Your affiant received training while at the police academy in crimes including, but not limited to:  various narcotics offenses, weapons and firearms offenses, domestic violence, child abuse, sexual exploitation of minors, theft, vehicle theft, burglary, fraud, identity theft, robbery, assault, aggravated assault and homicide.  After graduating the academy, your affiant held positions at the Los Angeles Police Department as a Patrol Officer, Bicycle Officer, and Traffic Accident Investigator.  In 2001, your affiant left the Los Angeles Police Department and joined the Fontana Police Department.  During your affiant’s time at the Fontana Police Department he has been a Patrol Officer, Field Training Officer, Firearms Instructor, Emergency Vehicle Operation Instructor (E.V.O.C), a Driving Simulator Instructor, a Driving Safety Instructor, a Force Options Simulator Instructor, and a Community Emergency Response Team Instructor (C.E.R.T).  Your affiant is currently a Police Corporal working as a Detective assigned to the Internet Crimes Against Children (ICAC) Task Force in the Investigations Unit.
