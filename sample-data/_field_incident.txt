I gave the user an undercover number. 
The user texted me on 05/02/2024 from telephone number (667)967-6512, the message read “Hi” when asked who it was
they stated;
Your potential new mom and dad
We continue messaging and I send them an undercover photograph. 
They also send me a photograph of a male and female standing in what appears to be the living room.
After talking it was learned that they lived in Maryland. 
05/03/2024
Deputy Bowman of the Alleghany County Sheriffs Office spoke with a male on the phone, Bowman was pretending to be
the juveniles mother. The talk was about letting the alleged Juvenile go to Maryland to live with them. During the
conversation, the male identified himself as “Al” who drives trucks and the wife as “Tammy” who works at a doctors
office. 
Al stated that they had an RV and enjoy traveling, Al sent multiple photos of his vehicles to my undercover number. 
In one of the photos was a yellow Chevrolet Car with Maryland Tags “5DF8068” and behind the vehicle is a Crusader
Camper, brownish in color.
In another photo shows a Silver Jeep.
In another photo was of a black Chevrolet pickup with Maryland Registration “42S571”, in the background is a house with
the bottom portion brick and the siding is a yellow in color.
I open sourced the phone number and found that it belonged to;
Albert Forand  Age 57, Born October 1966
109 Hammarlee Rd. Glen Burnie, MD 21060
I typed the address into google and it provided a street view of the address which match the house in the photograph of
the black pickup, also in street view of google maps it showed what appeared to be the same black pickup truck. 
On 05/06/2024 I had Deputy Swoope of the Alleghany County Sheriff’s Office call the number and spoke with a male.
Swoope was pretending to be the underage girl. He stated to Swoope that he was wanting me to become a sister-wife.
 On 05/15/2024 I, Investigator Morris, had Deputy Swoope of the Alleghany County Sheriff’s Office call the male. During
the phone call he mentioned doing “Adult things” and wanted to have “Sex”, he also talked about birth control and asked
about past sexual relationships juvenile may have had. 
Later, I asked him what his last name was in which he stated “Forand” 
On 05/18/2024 Forand traveled to Covington, VA to meet the alleged juvenile to take back to Maryland. Forand was driving
a Silver Jeep Bearing Maryland Tags 5BY3055. In the vehicle was Albert Forand, Tamara Forand and their daughter Katie
Forand. 
Albert Forand admitted to talking to who he thought was a 14 year old female. He confessed that he was going to have the
juvenile as a “Daughter-wife” then marry her when she turned 18. 
Mr. Forand was taken before a magistrate where i received an arrest warrant for Code of Virginia 18.2-374.3(C) Use of
communication systems to facilitate certain offenses against minors. 
Forand was held with no bond.
