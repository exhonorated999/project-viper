Purpose-Built ICAC Center

A complete Internet Crimes Against Children intelligence workflow.

Turn a pile of NCMEC CyberTips into an organized, cross-referenced, court-ready intelligence store — entirely on your own machine.

icac_dashboard — timeline, provider intel, assignment queue & export

From CyberTip pile to prioritized queue.

  • Bulk-import CyberTips — drop in the ZIPs, and VIPER parses every PDF automatically: provider, incident type, dates, identifiers.
  • Cross-reference identifiers — phones, IPs and ESP user IDs are extracted and linked, surfacing repeat suspects across separate tips.
  • Categorize by severity — sort and triage by NCMEC category (A1, A2, B1…) so the worst rises to the top first.
  • Categorize by media volume — rank tips by number of provided files, so high-contraband cases don't get buried.
  • Assign over LAN — route a tip to an investigator by CyberTip number only; the media never moves.

Wilson warrants, linked to the batch.

Working a bulk download from the ICAC Data System? Attach the signed Wilson warrant PDF right in the staging tray — it's saved and permanently linked to every CyberTip in that batch.

  • Attach once, cover the batch — one warrant applies to the whole import.
  • Preserved for recall — PDFs are stored in a dedicated back-end folder, retrievable any time.
  • Embedded IDS browser — pull ZIPs from ICACdatasystem.com into staging without leaving the app.
